Design the Future of Domestic Robotics

This project explores new design proposals for a general-purpose social home robot that enhances domestic well-being and mental health post-pandemic. These robots are companions designed for natural human-robot interaction and shaped through aesthetic coding to foster trust and acceptance.

Proposal 1: A compact, playful semi-humanoid robot with expressive eyes, designed to emotionally support and assist with simple domestic tasks.

Proposal 2: A functional and friendly service bot with a cylindrical body, ideal for elder care and information assistance in a home setting.

Proposal 3: A sleek humanoid helper bot, designed for advanced interaction and physical assistance with a futuristic yet approachable presence.
